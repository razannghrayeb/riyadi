About RB Leipzig

Welcome to RB Leipzig's official website. We are a professional football club based in Leipzig, Germany. Founded in 2009, RB Leipzig has quickly risen to prominence in German football.

At RB Leipzig, we are committed to excellence both on and off the field. Our mission is to entertain and inspire fans through our exciting style of football while representing the city of Leipzig with pride.
